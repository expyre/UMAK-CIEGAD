ABOUT US

The Center for Inclusive Education Gender and Development was established with the primary purpose of providing free, appropriate, quality, and industry driven education to persons with disabilities and to those who are experiencing difficulties due to some personal preferences and reasons. The center allows LSENS to learn alongside with regular students without them being segregated or confined in a self-contained environment. With the merging of the Center for Gender and Development and Center or Inclusive Education, the thrust of making a difference for those who are different now makes more sense. In essence, this center ensures to capacitate every student to participate and take his/her role as a productive member of any society regardless of any personal or physical limitations, ethnicity, cultural background or other limitations brought about by their situations.


This center also guarantees that laws and policies concerning the rights and privileges of all students but not limited to the above mentioned sector are taken into consideration to address diversity, thus resulting to an inclusive and gender responsive and inclusive university and city. As mandated by the University of Makati, the vital goal of the center is the full mainstreaming of our graduates for functionality and independent living.Â 

OBJECTIVES:

1. To provide appropriate tertiary education, technical or vocational training to Persons With Disabilities that will empower them to secure gainful employment and independent living.


2. To make Persons with Disabilities realize their full potential and with proper and timely intervention, they can be part of an active community who can contribute to the progress of the society.


3. To form alliances and linkages with public and private organizations for the inclusion of qualified Persons with Disabilities in their workforce and enjoy the same benefits as provided for by prevailing laws and policies.


4. To encourage the participation of the community, industries and other agencies in programs and other activities geared towards the total welfare and development of Person with Disabilities.


5. For organization and industries to adhere to accessibility requirements for Person with Disabilities and to make necessary modifications in preparation for the accommodation of Persons with Disabilities.


6. To prepare the University of Makati community in the accommodation of Person With Disability and to fully realize the ideals of inclusive education.

VISION

The center envisions the full integration of persons with or without disabilities into the mainstreamed society through appropriate, quality, skills based and industry driven tertiary education.


MISSION

To achieve our vision, the center vows to assist and to prepare persons with or without disabilities in their pursuit of higher education with strengthened transition program that will redound to functionality, empowerment and independent living.


CORE VALUES

(F.I.R.S.T)


1. F - FAIR

Development for all without exception through a carefully crafted person-centered career plan


2. I - Inclusive

All students learn and live together in an environment of harmony and equality


3. R - Resilient

Nothing can hinder the achievement of dreams and goals of gaining education despite differences and difficulties


4. S - Service Oriented

Determined to help beyond graduation and to give back the same opportunities to others


5. T - Transformative

Developmental approach in pursuing educational goals for functionality and independent living
